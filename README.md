## About Me

I am a Transportation Engineer and academic based in Nepal, specializing in **traffic engineering, road safety, and transport planning**. With over six years of teaching experience across multiple engineering colleges in Kathmandu Valley, I combine hands-on consultancy work with active research and publication.

I hold an **M.Sc. in Transportation Engineering** from Tribhuvan University (program topper, 88.93%) and an **MBA in Corporate Leadership** (GPA 3.94), and I am currently an **Assistant Professor and Head of Department** at Aryan School of Engineering and a faculty member in the Master's program at Universal Engineering & Science College.
